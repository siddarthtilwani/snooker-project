Introduction to Snooker

Snooker is a cue sport played on a large, rectangular table with six pockets. The goal of the game is to score more points than your opponent by pocketing balls with your cue stick. Snooker is a challenging and rewarding game that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. If you're looking for a new sport to try, we encourage you to give snooker a shot!
